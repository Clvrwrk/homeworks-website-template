## 2. Writing Style

**Default Tone:**  
`[e.g., Professional, Friendly, Authoritative, Direct]`

**Voice Notes:**  
*Describe the personality and stylistic guidelines for the copy.*
- **Direction:** "Trusted Craftsman" — Confident Editorial with Warmth.
- The tone should be authoritative but approachable, professional but not corporate.
- Use active voice and focus on solving the customer's problem (e.g., "Fix This Problem").
- Emphasize trust signals naturally in the copy (insured, background-checked, guaranteed).
- Keep paragraphs short and scannable.

**Words/Phrases to Avoid:**  
- Corporate jargon
- Passive language
- "Cheap" or "Discount" (use "Value" or "Transparent Pricing" instead)
- Filler words
- Overused SEO phrases
- *Note: Avoid using zip codes in customer-facing copy; homeowners think in terms of cities and neighborhoods.*
